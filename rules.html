Rules

Progressing through the game: 

You’ll start by opening puzzles 1-3. When you solve a puzzle, input your answer into https://hlehv.github.io/unbirthday.github.io/ (or use the QR code below). You’ll then get target for the scavenger hunt you must take a picture of. All team members must be in the picture.

Once you’ve sent your picture and one of the mods thumbs it up, you can open the next envelope in the sequence.

Every 30 minutes another puzzle will unlock for all teams regardless of how many puzzles they’ve solved. We’ll message all teams to let them know the puzzle has unlocked.

When 7pm hits, the team with the most points wins.

Bonus pictures:

These are items you can take a picture of at any time once each for 1 point

  • Picture of a waymo
  • Someone eating ice cream
  • A rose bush
  • A photo of you picking up a piece of litter and tossing it
  • Selfie with Hollis
  • Selfie with Chris
  • Riot games reference (character from a game, a sign with Riot Games in it, etc.)
  • A Japanese flag

Photo contests:

Photo contests will be judged by Hollis and Chris, and only one team will win each one.

  • Cutest dog
  • Funniest photo
  • Most expensive boba
  • Most expensive car
  • Most expensive item (tag must be in the image)

Points:

  • Solving a puzzle: 5 points
  • Sending a picture (of the correct item, with all team members): 5 points
  • Bonus picture: 1 point
  • Winning a picture contest: 3 points
  • Using a hint: -1 points

Using hints:

Send a message asking for a hint in your team’s discord channel and Chris or Hollis will respond ASAP. Include the puzzle you’re on and how far you’ve gotten. You can also call Hollis at 917-734-2998.


Google and AI:

You can use google. You can use AI as long as you’re not sending AI the puzzle or pieces of it and asking it to solve it. Essentially use it in the same way you’d use google - to look things up. If you have questions on this ask Hollis or Chris.

Answers:

The answer will be a word, name or phrase. Some puzzles may have a partial solution before the final solution. You can submit partial solutions on the website and it may give you a hint on how to find the final solution. Do not inspect the website, this will never be necessary to solve a puzzle and will spoil you.
